200 years of History

Created by D. João VI, on June 6, 1818 and initially, based in Campo de Sant'Ana, it served to serve the interests of promoting the country's cultural and economic progress.

As a university museum, it has an academic and scientific profile. Its exhibitions result from the history of the institution and the excellence of its research and teaching activities, fulfilling the primary purpose of producing and disseminating knowledge in the areas of natural and anthropological sciences.
